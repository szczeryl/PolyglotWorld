Let's test your knowledge for numbers between 1 and 10!







For numbers between 11 to 19, combine the 2nd digit + "belas". For example, 16 becomes "enam belas". However, do note that "satu" is shortened to "se", hence 11 is "sebelas".





For 2 digit numbers 20 and above, we say the first number, followed by "puluh", then the second number. 26 will be "dua puluh enam" for instance and eighty will be "lapan puluh". Give the questions below a try!





"Ratus" is the word for "hundred". Hence, 128 would be "seratus dua puluh lapan" and 821 will be "lapan ratus dua puluh satu". With this, give the questions below a try!





But of course, a string of numbers can be said individually too. For example, 29 may be simply shortened to "dua sembilan".

The word for thousand is "ribu" and the word for million is "juta". You will probably not come across numbers so huge unless you were to travel to Indonesia though. Do however note that 8 is " delapan" in Indonesian.
